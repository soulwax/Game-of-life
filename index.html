Conway's Game of Life

Instructions:

  • Click cells to toggle them on/off (only when simulation is stopped)
  • Use "Double Size" and "Half Size" buttons to change the grid dimensions based on powers of 2
  • Adjust the speed slider to control the simulation speed
  • Reset clears the grid
  • Random creates a random pattern
  • Add Glider places a glider pattern that will move diagonally

Rules:

  1. Any live cell with fewer than two neighbors dies (underpopulation)
  2. Any live cell with two or three neighbors survives
  3. Any live cell with more than three neighbors dies (overpopulation)
  4. Any dead cell with exactly three neighbors becomes alive (reproduction)
